Our Dream

Palmdream® is more than just coconut water; it’s a sweeter way to celebrate life. Embracing our present without compromising our generation’s future. We believe in supporting and advocating sustainability for our planet, social responsibility for our people and a healthier approach for our lives. It all starts with our farmers, our teams and each and every person involved in bringing you our dreamy taste experience.

We are committed to adhering to best practices for GMO avoidance ensuring that our coconut water meets the Non-GMO Project Standards.

Palmdream® has also pledged to reduce to zero its carbon footprint impact to our world by 2025.

With all our projects and commitments, we truly believe this is how we can transform our dream into reality!
